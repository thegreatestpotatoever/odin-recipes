Cereal from the box

some cereal from the box

Ingredients

  • Cereal, in a box.

Steps

  1. Check the space where you keep your food and determine whether you have a box of cereal. If you do, open the box and eat some of the cereal from the box.
  2. If you do not have a box of cereal in your vicinity, assess whether you have enough money to buy a box. If you do, find a way to the store that sells cereal and buy a box. Enjoy your cereal.
  3. If you do not have cereal and you do not have money, consider asking if someone will buy you a box of cereal. If you are presented with a box of cereal, express gratitude and enjoy your cereal.
